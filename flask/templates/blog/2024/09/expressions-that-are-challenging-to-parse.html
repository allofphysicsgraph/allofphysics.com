Expressions that are challenging to parse

Published 2024-09-08T01:26:00.006Z by Physics Derivation Graph

Just a symbol: $$ A $$ A symbol that uses Latex: $$ \vec{A} $$ An expression that has a left and right side: $$ A = B $$ An expression with multiple terms: $$ A + B = C $$ More complicated multi-term expressions that use Latex: $$ \vec{A} = u \hat{x} + v \hat{y} $$ $$ A^u + \frac{Y}{3} + \int B dx + \sum_{z=1}^{n} c_k = D f(y) - G|_0^{\inf} $$ An expression involving a matrix: $$ \hat{A} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3\\ a & b & c \end{bmatrix} $$ An expression with a condition: $$ A = x y \mbox{when } x>0 $$ $$ f(x) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} x^2 & \mbox{when } x \geq 0 \\ x & \mbox{when } x < 0 \end{array} \right. $$ $$ A = x y \mbox{where } x \in \Integer $$ $$ A = x \mbox{when } x << 3 $$ $$ x = \pm \sqrt{a^2 - 4} $$

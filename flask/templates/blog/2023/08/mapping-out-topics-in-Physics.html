If we constructed a map of constraints on applicability of theories, how many dimensions would be needed and what would the dimensions be?

  • Newtonian Mechanics
    • spatial size between thousands of kilometers and millimeters
    • temporal duration between thousands of years and milliseconds
    • speed of object between motionless and thousands of meters per second
  • Electrodynamics, governed by Maxwell's equations
  • General relativity
  • Special Relativity
  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Relativistic Quantum Mechanics
